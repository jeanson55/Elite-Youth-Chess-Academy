Welcome

    Welcome to Elite Youth Chess Academy, a weekend training program based at UMaT Campus, Tarkwa, where young minds learn to think critically, act strategically, and play confidently. Whether your child is a complete beginner or already knows the basics, we provide a fun, structured environment to grow through the beautiful game of chess.

Our Mission

    To empower Ghanaian teens through chess — building focus, discipline, creativity, and confidence both on and off the board.
